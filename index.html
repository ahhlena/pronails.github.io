About

Pro Nails is a small family business in Spindale, North Carolina. It has been run by Kim and Lee for over 20 years. Pro Nails specializes in nail services including manicures, pedicures, and hand-drawn designs. Pro Nails also provides appointment based cosmetic services such as microblading and lashes. Kim and Lee are dedicated to providing their clients with a high quality services in a family-friendly environment.
